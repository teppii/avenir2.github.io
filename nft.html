What is NFT?

NFT is a digital asset that represents real-world objects like
art, music, in-game items and videos. They are bought and
sold online, frequently with cryptocurrency, and they are
generally encoded with the same underlying software.

What is NFT art?

NFT Art is a form of NFT in the field of art, created by
artists. For the field of art, collectors or investors are willing
to spend large amounts of money to identify the origin as
well as identify the features of a painting or a work of art.

How to buy NFT?

Most of today's NFTs are purchased with Ether (ETH). It can be easily
converted to USD on exchanges like Coinbase and Binance.

To buy NFT art, you need a digital wallet. Once you have a compatible
crypto wallet, you will need to fund it with Ether and connect to the desired
NFT marketplace.

If you don't have a non-custodial Ethereum wallet, you can create one
using MetaMask or WalletConnect. This is what we will be using in our
tutorial. Next, you need to choose a market of your choice.

How to sell on Opensea?

Select the NFT you would like to sell from your wallet. If you don't have an
NFT available to sell, check out our create an NFT tutorial to get started.

Select Sell on the top right to be taken to the listing page. You'll be taken
to the listing page, where you can choose the price and type of sale.
