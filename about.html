About us

Avenir (Avenir in French means future) is an art center located in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. Avenir was built with the purpose in mind for art enthusiasts in general and especially contemporary art.

Avenir has regular exhibitions (online, offline exhibitions), artist talks and workshops.

Our mission

With the desire to spread new artistic values, Avenir wishes to be able to inspire and help the growing community of Vietnamese artists and make constructive contributions to social life.
